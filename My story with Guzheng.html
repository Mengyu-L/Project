My Story with Guzheng

I have been trained playing “Guzheng”, a traditional Chinese musical instrument, for more than 10 years, and has a deep understanding of Chinese classic music culture. Talk about the aesthetic in music, I believe that Chinese music has Harmony as it’s core culture, the key words are ideal, nothingness, ethereal, charm, deep thinking, conception, intuition and respect for human race.
