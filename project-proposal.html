1. What is your project about?

I learned Guzheng for 15 years, I really want to share this traditional Chinese Instrument to the Western Country. I will include the history of Guzheng, different style of Guzheng, famous songs of Guzheng and my story with Guzheng.

2. What is the objective of your website? (Also read as why would this site be important?)

My objective in the website is to introduce Guzheng this traditional chinese instrument and let more people in Western countries know about chinese instrument.

3. Who is your target audience set?

My target audience is whoever interested in learning different culture and love music

4. What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

I will be including the history of Guzheng,different style of Guzheng, famous songs of Guzheng and my story with Guzheng. In these pages I will be using many images and some videos of Guzheng.
